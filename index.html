About Us

Welcome to Dvin Restaurant, where the culinary traditions of the Caucasus region come alive to tantalize your taste buds. Situated in the heart of the city, Dvin offers a dining experience that combines authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and a vibrant ambiance.

Our Story:
At Dvin, we take pride in our rich heritage and culinary roots that trace back to the ancient land of the Caucasus. Named after the historic city of Dvin, once a prosperous capital of Armenia, our restaurant is a tribute to the time-honored recipes and techniques passed down through generations.

The founders, a group of passionate food enthusiasts with a shared love for the diverse and flavorful dishes of the Caucasus, embarked on a journey to create a dining haven that celebrates the region's gastronomic delights. With a dedication to preserving traditional flavors while adding a modern twist, Dvin was born, and we've been serving culinary magic ever since.
